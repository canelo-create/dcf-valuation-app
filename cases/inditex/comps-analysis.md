## Key Findings

### Inditex premium to peer median

| Multiple | Inditex | Peer Median | Premium / (Discount) |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| EV/Sales | 3.66x | 1.88x | +95% |
| EV/EBITDA | 13.17x | 9.42x | +40% |
| P/E (TTM) | 24.30x | 18.75x | +30% |

### Premium justification

1. **Best in class margins.** EBITDA margin 23.6% vs peer median 13.4% (75% higher). Op margin 20.2% vs peer median 11.1% (82% higher).
2. **Scale + capital efficiency.** Net cash position EUR 5 B vs peers mostly net debt.
3. **Lower beta (0.92).** Less cyclical than peers (Gap 2.08, Next 1.05).
4. **Brand portfolio diversification.** 7 brands vs single brand pure-plays.
5. **Supply chain moat.** Spain proximity manufacturing model (vs Asia for HM, Uniqlo) gives speed advantage.

### Fast Retailing anomaly

Trades at 24.6x EV/EBITDA vs Inditex 13.2x despite LOWER margins (17.6% op vs 20.2%). Reasons:
- Asia growth premium (Uniqlo China, India expansion thesis)
- Lower interest rate environment Japan inflates multiples
- Lower share count, less liquid float
- Possible overvalued (consensus may agree, P/E fwd 42.9x demanding)

### Bottom quartile (Gap, ANF) reflect:
- US retail structural risk (mall traffic decline)
- Margin compression vs European/Japanese peers
- High beta (Gap 2.08 — extreme cyclical)
- Implied "value trap" or genuine cheapness — DCF will disambiguate

## Limitations

1. Single point in time TTM figures, no historical trend
2. FX cross-currency comparison: multiples currency-neutral ✓, but margins compared in local accounting standards (IFRS vs US GAAP). H&M, Inditex, Fast Retail IFRS. Gap, ANF US GAAP. Material diff: lease accounting (IFRS 16 capitalizes all leases as debt/EBITDA, US GAAP similar post ASC 842 since 2019, broadly comparable now).
3. Source stockanalysis.com aggregates from filings, not primary. Re-pull from 20-F/annual reports for production deliverable.
4. Fast Retailing fiscal year ends Aug 31 (TTM ends Feb 28 2026 approx); others mostly Jan-Dec or Jan-end (Inditex). 4-6 month period offset.
5. Same-store sales (LFL) not in peer set yet, retail key metric. Add for v2.
